About Evodroid

Evodroid is an Android application for b2evolution - a powerful free blog/CMS engine.

This app works with b2evolution v5.0 and higher. As of July 2012 b2evolution v5.0 has not been released to the public yet.

Please report bugs and submit your suggestions to the bug tracker.
